Spaghetti Carbonara

A traditional Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti tossed in a rich and creamy sauce made from eggs and Parmesan cheese. Infused with the smoky flavor of crispy pancetta, and seasoned with freshly ground black pepper, this pasta dish is both simple and indulgent. Finished with a sprinkling of extra cheese and a touch of parsley, our Spaghetti Carbonara offers a perfect blend of savory and creamy notes that will delight your taste buds and leave you craving more.



Category:

savoury

Duration:

50 min

Ingredients:

200g spaghetti, 100g pancetta, 2 large eggs, 50g pecorino cheese, 50g parmesan, 2 plump garlic cloves - peeled and left whole, 50g unsalted butter, sea salt and freshly grated black pepper

Preparations and Instructions:

Put a large saucepan of water on to boil. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind. Beat the eggs in a medium bowl, season with a little freshly grated black pepper and set everything aside. Add the spaghetti to the water and cook according to the pack instructions. Squash the garlic with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it. Melt the butter in a deep frying pan, then add the pancetta and garlic. Leave to cook on a medium heat for about 5 minutes, stirring often, until the pancetta is golden and crisp. Take the pan of spaghetti off the heat. Quickly pour in the eggs and pancetta, and toss together. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese and a grating of black pepper.
