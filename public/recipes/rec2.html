Spaghetti Carbonara

A traditional Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti tossed in a rich and creamy sauce made from eggs and Parmesan cheese. Infused with the smoky flavor of crispy pancetta, and seasoned with freshly ground black pepper, this pasta dish is both simple and indulgent. Finished with a sprinkling of extra cheese and a touch of parsley, our Spaghetti Carbonara offers a perfect blend of savory and creamy notes that will delight your taste buds and leave you craving more.



Category:

Pasta

Duration:

30 Minutes

Ingredients:

200g spaghetti 100g pancetta 2 large eggs 50g pecorino cheese 50g parmesan 2 plump garlic cloves - peeled and left whole

Preparations and Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Brown the ground beef in a pan over medium heat. Drain any excess fat. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened. Arrange taco shells in a baking dish. Spoon the beef mixture into each shell. Sprinkle cheese over the beef-filled shells. Bake for 10-12 minutes until cheese is melted and shells are crispy. Add your favorite toppings and serve hot.
