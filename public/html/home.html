Ready to cook?

Browse through our collection of delicious yet easy-to-make recipes that bring joy to your tastebuds. Whether you're having a small dinner or a fun party, discover the perfect recipe that matches your grandeur — We'll also make sure your guests return for the food.

Baked Tacos


Crispy oven-baked Tacos — Made with crunchy hard taco shells filled with perfectly seasoned ground beef and a blend of taco seasoning.
Topped with a generous layer of melted cheese, fresh pico de gallo, a dollop of creamy sour cream, and slices of ripe avocado. These tacos
offer a delightful combination of textures and flavors that will elevate your taco night to a whole new level. Enjoy a satisfying and delicious
meal that's easy to prepare and guaranteed to impress your family and friends.

Spaghetti Carbonara


A traditional Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti tossed in a rich and creamy sauce made from eggs and Parmesan cheese.
Infused with the smoky flavor of crispy pancetta, and seasoned with freshly ground black pepper, this pasta dish is both simple and indulgent.
Finished with a sprinkling of extra cheese and a touch of parsley, our Spaghetti Carbonara offers a perfect blend of savory and creamy notes that
will delight your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Nasi Goreng


Savory Nasi Goreng — This Indonesian fried rice features fragrant jasmine rice stir-fried with garlic, shallots, and soy sauce. Mixed with tender
chicken, succulent shrimp, and fresh vegetables, it's topped with a fried egg, crispy shallots, and tangy pickles. A burst of authentic flavors,
perfect for a quick dinner or a special treat.
